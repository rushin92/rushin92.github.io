WIndows 11 System Requirements


NOTE:This Does Not Apply To Windows On ARM

CPU: 1 gigahertz (GHz) or faster with 2 or more cores and must be an Intel Core 8th Gen Or Newer/Amd Ryzen 3rd Gen Or Newer*

RAM: 4 gigabyte (GB)

64 GB or larger storage device

GPU: Compatible with DirectX 12 or later with WDDM 2.0 driver

UEFI, Secure Boot capable

Trusted Platform Module (TPM) version 2.0
*Some EPYC,Xeon,Celeron,Pentium,Athlon,Threadripper CPU's are also supported
